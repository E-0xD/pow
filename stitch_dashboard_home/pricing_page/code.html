Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our plans.

Can I cancel my subscription? expand_more

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Your plan will remain active until the end of the current billing cycle, and you will not be charged again.

What happens if I downgrade? expand_more

When you downgrade, you will retain access to your current plan's features until the end of your billing period. After that, your account will be moved to the new, lower-tier plan, and some features may become unavailable.

Do you offer a non-profit discount? expand_more

We are happy to support non-profit organizations. Please contact our sales team with your organization's details to learn more about our special pricing for non-profits.

Is there a trial for the Pro plan? expand_more

You can start with our Free plan to get a feel for the platform. When you're ready, you can upgrade to the Pro plan. We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied.
